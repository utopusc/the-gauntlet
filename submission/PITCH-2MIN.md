# THE GAUNTLET — 2-Minute Elevator Pitch Video Script

**Format:** Slide-by-slide / shot-by-shot. ~120 seconds. Built to be shared on its own (works muted — every line of voiceover is mirrored in on-screen text).
**Tone:** Fast, confident, a little dangerous. Energy of a fight-game trailer, substance of a seed deck.

**Asset legend:** `[VO]` = voiceover · `[OST]` = on-screen text · `[SHOT]` = visual / shot direction.

---

### SLIDE 1 — COLD OPEN (0:00–0:10)
**[SHOT]** Black screen. A single blinking cursor. We type live: `An app that uses AI to...` then it cuts off. Hard cut to a red ARENA gate slamming shut behind the text.
**[VO]** "Every founder thinks their idea is fundable. Almost none of them survive the first three questions."
**[OST]** *You have ONE pitch. Most founders waste it.*

---

### SLIDE 2 — THE PROBLEM (0:10–0:28)
**[SHOT]** Split screen. Left: a nervous founder in a glassy VC office. Right: the VC's polite, dead-eyed "Interesting — keep us posted." Then a graveyard of greyed-out startup logos slides past.
**[VO]** "VCs never tell you the real reason they passed. No moat. No go-to-market math. No idea what your CAC is. You find out live, under pressure, when it's already too late."
**[OST]** *The "no" is polite. The reason is hidden. The shot is gone.*

---

### SLIDE 3 — THE TURN (0:28–0:40)
**[SHOT]** The graveyard ignites into a neon fighting-game arena. Title card SLAMS in with screen-shake.
**[VO]** "So we built the practice partner that actually fights back. This is THE GAUNTLET."
**[OST]** **THE GAUNTLET** — *The fundraising flight simulator.* · *Powered by Gemini.*

---

### SLIDE 4 — HOW IT WORKS (0:40–1:05)
**[SHOT]** UI walkthrough. Founder types one idea into a single input. Three boss portraits light up in sequence with HP bars: **MARKET · TECH · GTM**. We see one exchange: founder answers, Gemini fires back a razor question, the Market Boss's HP bar visibly drops.
**[VO]** "Type your idea. Then fight three AI investor bosses, powered by Gemini. The Market boss attacks your timing and your moat. The Tech boss attacks what breaks at scale. The GTM boss attacks your numbers. Sharp answers drain their health. Hand-waving drains yours."
**[OST]** *3 bosses. Real questions. Real HP. Gemini scores every word.*

---

### SLIDE 5 — THE PAYOFF (1:05–1:25)
**[SHOT]** Final boss HP hits zero — confetti, "VICTORY." A premium **Term Sheet card** flips into frame: big number **FUNDABILITY: 84/100**, strengths, fatal gaps, "the question you ducked." A finger taps SHARE.
**[VO]** "Survive all three and you walk out with a fundability verdict — a shareable Term Sheet card. Your score. Your strengths. The exact gap a real VC would have killed you on."
**[OST]** **FUNDABILITY 84/100** · *Strengths · Fatal gaps · Share your verdict.*

---

### SLIDE 6 — WHY NOW + MARKET (1:25–1:45)
**[SHOT]** Quick montage: a YC-style demo-day room, a university pitch night, a phone feed full of "I scored 84" verdict cards spreading.
**[VO]** "AI made starting a company nearly free — so the bottleneck moved to being fundable. We start with founders and demo-day cohorts, then license the readiness engine to accelerators and universities as a standardized assessment. Every play ships a card built to be posted."
**[OST]** *Wedge: viral founder game → B2B founder-readiness assessment.*

---

### SLIDE 7 — THE ASK / CLOSE (1:45–2:00)
**[SHOT]** Back to the arena gate, now open, light pouring out. Logo + URL + a single CTA button pulsing.
**[VO]** "We're looking for accelerators to pilot THE GAUNTLET as cohort prep — and partners to back the wedge. Think your idea's fundable? Prove it. Step into the Gauntlet."
**[OST]** **Step into the Gauntlet.** · *the-gauntlet · Powered by Gemini · GDG Stanford × Red Bull Basement*

**Editing notes for virality:** First 3 seconds must show the typed idea + slamming gate (scroll-stopper). The 84/100 card reveal at 1:05 is the moment people screenshot — hold it a full beat. End on a dare, not a thank-you.
